Overview

This campaign focused on improving lead quality while reducing wasted ad spend. The account had active campaigns but lacked efficiency and targeting precision. Our goal was to optimize performance and scale conversions sustainably.

Before vs After Optimization

Before

  • Higher CPA
  • Wasted spend on irrelevant queries
  • Weak audience targeting
  • Limited keyword coverage

After

  • CPA reduced by $24
  • More qualified traffic
  • Improved targeting accuracy
  • Higher conversion efficiency

Challenges

  • High cost per conversion
  • Incorrect location targeting
  • Irrelevant traffic
  • Low optimization frequency

Strategy

  • Keyword expansion (high intent)
  • Search term cleanup
  • Audience targeting added
  • Location targeting fixed

Results

  • 83 total conversions generated
  • Cost reduced by $1,624
  • CPA improved to $58.76
  • Conversion volume increased
🚀 Strong improvement in efficiency — lower cost, higher output.

Performance Trend

Conclusion

By refining campaign structure, targeting, and keyword strategy, the account achieved better performance with reduced costs. This approach ensured scalable and consistent lead generation moving forward.
