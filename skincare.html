Google Ads Growth for Skincare Brand

Performance Marketing Case Study (Feb–Apr 2024 vs Sep–Nov 2023)

-14.4%

+28.1%

Challenge

The brand was generating traffic but lacked efficiency in converting users into paying customers. High clicks with lower return indicated poor targeting and wasted ad spend.

Strategy

Rebuilt Google Ads campaigns focusing on high-intent keywords and removed low-performing segments. Optimized bidding strategies, improved audience targeting, and implemented conversion tracking. Reduced irrelevant traffic while increasing quality leads.

Execution

Structured campaigns into focused ad groups, improved ad copy for better CTR, and used extensions to increase visibility. Continuous A/B testing and bid adjustments were implemented to maximize ROI.

Results

Despite a drop in clicks, overall campaign efficiency improved significantly. Conversions increased by 53%, ROAS improved by 30%, and conversion value rose by 28%, indicating higher quality traffic and better budget utilization.
